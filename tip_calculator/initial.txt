Tip calculator - what it needs to do
- User enters a bill amount
- user selects a tip percentage (10%, 15%, 20%, 25%)
- user enters number of people splitting the bill
- app calculates and displays:
    - tip amount per person
    - total per person

Before any code - questions to answer

1. The math:
If the bill is $120, tip is 15%, and 3 people are splitting it:

What is the tip amount in dollars?
First 15% of 120 is 15 divided by 100 which is 0.15 then we multiply 0.15 by 120 which is 18 $. total with the 18$ added would be 138

What does each person pay in total?
138 divided by 3 is 46$. Each person pays 46$.
